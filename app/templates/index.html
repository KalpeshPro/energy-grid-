Key Insights

What the Data
Tells Us

📈
Growth

5.1% Annual Demand Rise

India's electricity consumption grows steadily at ~5% per year, driven by urbanisation, industrial expansion, and rising living standards across Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.

☀️
Renewables

Solar on Track for 37% by 2030

India's solar installation pace outpaces government targets. By 2026, renewables are forecast to cover 34% of total generation — displacing coal faster than expected.

🌡️
Climate

Summer Peaks Are Intensifying

April–June demand spikes correlate tightly with temperature anomalies. Model sensitivity shows 1°C above normal adds ~3,200 MW to peak load.

⚡
Grid

North & West Drive National Growth

Northern and Western regions account for 55% of forecast demand growth through 2026, with industrial corridors in UP, Gujarat, and Maharashtra as primary drivers.

🏭
Industry

Peak Hours Shifting with EVs

Electric vehicle adoption is reshaping evening peak demand profiles. By 2026, the 9–11 PM EV charging load could add 8–12 GW to nightly grid requirements.

🔋
Storage

Storage Becomes Critical by 2026

As renewable share crosses 30%, grid-scale storage becomes essential. Battery capacity needs to triple from current 1.2 GW to handle variability.
